Rankings with a Grain of Salt

We try to present the bigger picture of CS school rankings using both a school-centric and an area-centric view. Ranking criterion is the same as used by CSRankings. Faculty listings are based on whether a faculty member has published in an area in the past 5 years. The app currently only supports US and European schools and rankings are calculated by looking through faculty publications year 2000 onwards.
